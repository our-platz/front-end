WHO WE ARE

Historically, the intellectuals in Ancient Greece enjoyed having discussions about the topics of politics, art, and philosophy. This is well-described in the Plato’s philosophical text, Symposium. This type of culture led to Plaza, Forum and Musenhof and eventually developed into the Salons of Early Modern France. The Salons played an integral role in the cultural and intellectual development of France, through a lot of discussions and artistic exchanges.

We desire to form this sort of ‘advanced’ community and have a great influence on the Metaverse and NFT market.

OUR-PLATZ

is a

community that leads the intellectual and artistic culture in the Metaverse through free discussions and exchanges.

(Platz means the “town square” in German)

Our ultimate goal is to trigger the popularization of the Metaverse. For that, we believe that there must be a leading community that lays a cultural foundation, which will be OUR-PLATZ. For the vitalization and solidarity of the community, we will continuously launch our unique NFT collections and improve aesthetic value of the NFT space and the metaverse. Various discussions and exchanges within the community will occur with the common goal of NFT branding. Our NFT brands will be indeed luxurious and sensuous and our community will be thus prestigious.

So basically, we are the “Return of the Salons” that create luxurious NFT brands.
