NFT BRANDS

Once we've successfully built a solid community through OUR-GENESIS, we will start creating 'NFT brands' in sequence. This is because it is necessary to make a common cause to develop and expand OUR-PLATZ consistently.

Each brand will be independent and the quantity, price and branding strategies will vary according to the art concept. Each brand will consist of one main artist and several sub-artists(if necessary), and they will be selected by the votes of OUR-GENESIS holders as well as OUR-TEAM's opinions.

We will make the most of

"Artists"

We will make the most of ‘Artists’ from our community, and create premium brands by accepting opinions from other roles on marketing or partnerships.

Once the minting is over, We will release various merchandises that fit the brand concept, mainly collaborating with fashion-related brands to combine OUR-PLATZ with the real world.

These superior brands will spread out all over the world, and harmonize to create the ‘OUR-PLATZ Universe’. We will create new values through our universe and eventually reinforce our community-power.
