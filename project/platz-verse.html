PLATZ-VERSE

Our final goal is

to realize OUR-PLATZ in the 3D metaverse.

We are not building our own 3D metaverse platform but we are holding a couple of metaverse real-estates where we will establish ‘squares’ for OUR-PLATZ. We are still investigating which metaverse real-estates to use. It can be real-estates that we are currently holding or we can purchase a new one. Official announcement will be made once we decide which metaverse platform we are going to build 'squares' for OUR-PLATZ.

In PLATZ-VERSE, like the Salons in Modern France, there will be beautiful music, numerous works of art on display, and a 'discussion square' with enthusiastic conversations and exchanges. Moreover, we will create a space where all OUR-PLATZ members can enjoy freedom by designing creative and surreal elements that were once in our imagination.

The design of the square we mentioned above will change according to the characteristics of each metaverse platform and the scale of our real estate, at the same time, we will do our best to create space for our ultimate goal, 'a community that leads the intellectual and artistic culture'. Ofcourse, we will collect opinions from every role, select representatives, and let them participate in the design directly to create a space where everyone can be satisfied.
