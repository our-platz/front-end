ROLE SYSTEM

OUR-PLATZ will build a contributive community where members can feel a sense of belonging, accomplishment, and liberation. For this, we’ve created something fun:

ROLE-SYSTEM.

Every member must select one of three roles:

'Artists’, ‘Scholars’

and

'innovators'.

1. Artists

Artists basically 'colorize' OUR-PLATZ. Based on the foundations from ‘Scholars’ and the strategies from ‘Innovators', they create promotional videos, SNS background pictures, theme music, and design merchandise, clothing and PLATZ-VERSE models in the future.
With their aesthetic sense, they also propose ideas about the continuous NFT brands under OUR-PLATZ, and their creative artworks and aesthetic value will bring a paradigm shift to the NFT space and the metaverse.

2. Scholars

Scholars figure out, analyze and discuss the fundamental issues to decide the ultimate identity and directions of OUR-PLATZ. Artists and innovators design and promote OUR-PLATZ based on the scholars' rational understandings and ideas, and scholars will judge their performance and feedback them. They will reinforce our unique identity through many research and discussions. OUR-TEAM will refer to their thoughts and write articles to post on our Medium, or other SNS.

3. Innovators

Innovators build specific marketing and branding strategies and plan the future-direction such as IRL events, or the metaverse to develop and lead OUR-PLATZ They will embrace the fundamental conceptions of scholars and cooperate with artists to spread OUR-PLATZ widely. They also deal with various events to ensure a lively community. As the name 'innovators' suggests, they will create new values and services in the NFT world, based on our directions and ideas. As a result, they are responsible for the elements on the surface of OUR-PLATZ and play an important role by vitalizing and promoting OUR-PLATZ.

It’s always their freedom to contribute according to the roles and our team will do our job regardless of their contributions. Don't worry, we are not giving the pressure to contribute for OUR-PLATZ to community member. A good community always let all the member to enjoy!

Nevertheless, if they somehow help us create our direction and value, we promise to reward them right with numerous methods. We will host various community events or present topics of discussion and participate in that thus ‘lead’ this ‘leading’ community. All the suggestions and contributions from every role participant are welcomed but will not be guaranteed to be applied.
