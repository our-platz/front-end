IN REAL LIFE

As one of OUR-PLATZ’s philosophy is to narrow the distance between real-life space and Virtual space, we are processing and planning partnerships with various companies, projects and individuals for real-life space as well. We will also seek solutions to overcome some important problems outside our community, and have various positive influences on the real space.

Planned works in real life (will be added more)

1. Partnership with professional videographers

We are planning to make theme videos which represent our vision, directions and community. Those videos will be on our SNS, Discord and anywhere we can show who-we-are. Also, we will produce different theme and commercial videos for each NFT brand under OUR-PLATZ.

2. Partnership with fashion brands and designers

Our NFT artworks and branding elements will be used in fashion. We are planning to have partnerships with fashion brands or individual fashion designers for designing qualitative fashion products.

3. Donating to charities by voting system

A good community always has a good impact on a society. Part of OUR-PLATZ’s funds will be used for donating to charities with our community members. There are a lot of matters to consider before choosing where to donate. Therefore, as transparency is one of the most important considerations for donations, we will listen to our community’s opinions on where they think our help is needed in society (specific charities cannot be recommended). Based on their opinions, we will make a candidate list and the final decision for choosing a charity will be made by voting results. In this process, only Genesis-NFT holders will be able to vote.

4. OUR-PLATZ offline pop-up gallery

OUR-PLATZ will hold a NFT art exhibitions in a pop-up galleries. Through the world-wide NFT art exhibitions, we can create value not only for ourselves but also for the overall NFT culture and public awareness about NFT. By designing pop-up gallery interiors tailored to the characteristics of each region, our exhibitions will be more friendly and enjoyable to the local public. Currently, several spots have been added to the list for consideration and will be officially announced once formalized.
